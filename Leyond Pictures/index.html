World Vitiligo Awareness Day

World Vitiligo Awareness Day is observed to raise awareness about vitiligo, a skin condition that affects millions of people worldwide. The day highlights the importance of understanding the challenges faced by those living with vitiligo and promotes inclusivity and support. Through global campaigns, it encourages acceptance and celebrates the beauty in diversity.
