World Vitiligo Awareness Day

  • Client:Dineo Mkhabela
  • Date:June 25, 2021
  • Categorie:Photography
  • Tags:beauty, awareness, photography

World Vitiligo Awareness Day is dedicated to increasing global awareness of vitiligo, a condition that causes the loss of skin pigmentation. It is a day to highlight the experiences of those affected by vitiligo and to promote education and understanding about this condition. Through various events and campaigns, we aim to support individuals with vitiligo and foster a more inclusive society.

Join us in spreading awareness and understanding of vitiligo. Our goal is to encourage open dialogue, support research, and improve the quality of life for those impacted by vitiligo.
